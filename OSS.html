On-Site Services

Experts with established mining technology experience in remote and harsh environments.

    FTP Solutions offers on-site services to fully realize the capability of the operational technology support model that the OTSC provides. The proposed personnel have specific on-site operational experience with autonomous and staffed mining technology operational networks. Our proposed model of internal staff improvement involves rotating our site based operational support team with our OTSC technical staff at our home office locations to ensure a familiarity of site-based processes and operations methodology to bring maximum value to our clients.

    Having experience developing operating processes and procedures, including implementation of these processes across major Tier 1 mining customers, the intrinsic knowledge of the FTP Solutions’ team is proven and well-documented. FTP Solutions’ site resources can augment a client’s existing network support team, or even replace the team as required during vacation or other resourcing constraints.

    This service is fully customizable to meet a client’s on-site shift requirements to ensure appropriate coverage is aligned with the defined operating model. This includes ensuring vacation coverage is maintained throughout a customer’s 24/7/365 operations.
