Integrated Management System


Visualise and optimise your IP and sensor networks with IMS.
A unique single-pane-of-glass platform to collate, manage and view real-time data.
A geographic information system (GIS) user interface (UI) provides a unique user experience and an unparalleled holistic view of your operations.

Cross Platform

IMS is a HTML5 based platform accessible via any web browser making it deployable in any corporate environment and on all devices.

Historical Data

Drag the time slider back and forth to see all your assets go backward and forward in time. Use it to visualise relationships and network connections as they happened, or use customisable dashboards to view historical performance.

Network

IMS gives technical users the power to manage and understand the entire network, through the single-pane-of-glass. View network topology and status of your 802.11, LTE or low powered mesh, like LoRa, Xbee or Zigbee (802.15.4).

Alerts

Custom Dashboards

Customizable reporting dashboards to provide deep insights into historical trends and data. Enable highly accurate root-cause analysis and incident identification allowing for accountable party reconciliation.
