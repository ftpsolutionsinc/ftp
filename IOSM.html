Integrated Operations Support Model

In depth operations support using advanced analytics and expert resources.

Key Features and Benefits

 
  • FTP Solutions staff rotate between providing on-site support and remote OTSC support
    • Intimate familiarity with the operations
    • Build deeper relationships with key stakeholders
    • Resources obtain site-specific training requirements for quick deployment as required across client sites

  • OTSC supports multiple mine sites running AHS application / network technology infrastructure
    • Deep knowledge base of common issues
    • Faster resolution times by applying this knowledge across multiple sites
    • Ability to circumvent issues before they occur (such as problems introduced with FW upgrades)

  • Follow-the-sun support model with OTSC in Calgary and Perth
    • Ability to scale for cost-effective 24/7/365 support
    • Always-on remote support available – not just an after-hours call service

  • OTSC lab replicates the mining operational technology network
    • Validate firmware upgrades and rollbacks in a controlled environment
    • Replicate issues for non-intrusive testing and fix experimentation
    • Test and validate new devices if required

  • Truly technology & vendor agnostic design and procurement services
    • Licensed to practice Engineering in Alberta
    • Transparent procurement services with pre-agreed mark-up

  • IMS expertise with OEM strategic partnerships
    • Ability to leverage the IMS tool to its fullest potential
    • Deeper understanding and visibility into AHS applications and network issues
    • Capability to add feature enhancements to IMS with endorsement / assistance from AHS vendors
