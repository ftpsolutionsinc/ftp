On-Site Technology Specialist

Our site-based technicians are leaders in the field of wireless networks and autonomous technology. Their exciting work consists of fault finding complex systems on some of the worlds largest machinery. Site-based roles are generally FIFO, allowing our employees a much needed rest after utilising their skills to solve real-world problems.

Working in the Operational Technology Support Centre, the Remote Operations Specialist utilizes cutting edge network management tools to identify, troubleshoot and resolve complex network issues in our customers environment. Our Remote Operations Staff handle the ins and outs of the customers network giving them the peace of mind to operate effectively and efficiently. This role is shift based work located in our HQ in Perth, or True North Solutions located in Calgary, Canada.

Network Engineer

Our Network Engineers work on highly complex designs and rollouts. The work can range from anywhere between a wireless network at Optus stadium to allow the contractors access to design files on the go, all the way to a complete network design for tier 1 mining companies implementing an Autonomous Haulage System at their mine site. Network Engineers at FTP enjoy a diverse range of projects and have helped build the company to what it is today.
